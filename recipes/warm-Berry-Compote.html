Warm Berry Compote

Description

This berry compote is made in a slow cooker with frozen berries and orange juice. It's a yummy dessert by itself or over ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups frozen mixed berries
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons finely grated orange zest
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch

Steps

  1. Stir frozen berries, sugar, orange juice, and orange zest together in slow cooker.
